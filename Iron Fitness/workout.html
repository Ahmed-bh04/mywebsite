Muscle Groups and Exercises

Muscle Group Description Example of the Exercise Videos
Chest Focuses on the upper chest and shoulders by pressing
dumbbells upward on an incline bench, helping build a fuller chest.		 Incline Dumbbell Press
Back A powerful full-body exercise that strengthens the back, glutes,
and hamstrings while improving overall strength and posture.		 Deadlifts
Legs Targets the legs and glutes by stepping forward and lowering the body,
great for building balance and lower-body strength.		 Lunges
Shoulders Shapes the shoulders by lifting dumbbells to the side,
emphasizing the lateral deltoid muscles for wider shoulders.		 Lateral Raises
Biceps Builds arm thickness by curling dumbbells with a neutral grip,
focusing on the brachialis muscle.		 Hammer Curls
Triceps Strengthens the triceps by lowering a barbell or dumbbells,
ideal for toning the back of the arms.		 Skull Crushers
Abs Enhances core strength and stability by twisting the torso
side-to-side while holding a weight or medicine ball.		 Russian Twists
